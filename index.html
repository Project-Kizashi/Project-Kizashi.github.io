Project-Kizashi

What is Project-Kizashi

Project-Kizashi is a aftermarket android firmware which is based on android and derieved form LineageOS and its forks. It focuses mainly on older android phones based on older linux kernels which cannot support latest android versions due to hardware restrictions.

It includes various tweaks to boost system performance and reliablity with a touch of customization and upto date with the latest Security patches released by google.
